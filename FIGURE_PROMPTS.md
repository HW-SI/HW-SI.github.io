# Figure Prompts

These prompts are for generating original, website-safe scientific illustrations. Prefer schematic clarity over photorealism. Avoid fake data axes, fake instrument logos, paper-like labels baked into the image, or anything that looks like an observational result unless it is based on real data you can cite.

## Microquasar Hero Figure

A clean scientific illustration of a stellar-mass black hole binary microquasar. Show a compact black hole accreting gas from a companion star through a luminous accretion disk, with two narrow relativistic jets emerging perpendicular to the disk. The style should be modern scientific editorial illustration, accurate proportions where possible, restrained colors, dark space background, no text labels, no logos, no exaggerated fantasy effects, suitable for an academic astrophysics website.

## Jet-Medium Interaction Schematic

A scientific schematic of a relativistic jet from a microquasar propagating into the surrounding interstellar medium. Show the jet termination region, a bow shock, turbulent interaction zone, and subtle particle trajectories representing cosmic rays and neutrinos. Use a clean academic visual style, muted colors, no labels, no fake data, no institutional logos, and enough negative space for a website layout.

## Multi-messenger Pipeline Figure

A horizontal scientific workflow illustration showing four connected stages: compact-object jet emission, jet termination and shocks, Galactic particle transport, and detector-space predictions for gamma rays and neutrinos. Use abstract but physically motivated visual elements, no text labels inside the image, no real observatory logos, high contrast, white or light background, suitable for adding captions below the image.

## Microquasar-SMBH Comparison Figure

A split-panel scientific illustration comparing a microquasar jet and a supermassive black-hole jet. Emphasize similar jet physics across very different mass scales. Use clean composition, realistic but schematic accretion flows, restrained colors, no text labels, no fake data, and no logos.
