Constraining hadronic processes in microquasar jets

My current work centers on microquasars, stellar-mass black holes with relativistic jets, using nearby sources such as V4641 Sgr as time-resolvable laboratories for jet physics.

I am building an end-to-end modeling pipeline that connects jet emission models, jet termination and interaction regions, Galactic particle transport, and detector-space predictions for gamma rays and neutrinos.
